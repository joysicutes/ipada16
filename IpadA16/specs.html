iPad and the Environment

iPad is designed to reduce environmental impact:

The iPad is thoughtfully designed to minimize its environmental impact. According to the iPad Product Environmental Report, it plays a key role in Apple's progress toward achieving its Apple 2030 goals. The device is made with 40% recycled content, and over 30% of the electricity used in its manufacturing comes from renewable sources.
